The BMW X1 is a compact luxury SUV that combines sporty performance with practicality. It features a premium interior, advanced technology, and efficient engines. Despite its small size, it offers ample cargo space and comfortable seating, making it ideal for city driving and long trips. The latest models also include hybrid and electric (iX1) versions for better efficiency.

The BMW X3 is a mid-size luxury SUV that blends strong performance, comfort, and advanced technology. It offers a spacious interior, powerful engine options, and smooth handling, making it perfect for both city and highway driving. The X3 also comes in petrol, diesel, and plug-in hybrid versions, providing a balance of luxury and practicality.

The BMW X5 is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its powerful performance, premium comfort, and advanced features. It offers strong engine options, spacious interiors, and high-end technology, making it ideal for families and long drives. The X5 combines off-road capability with a refined driving experience, and is also available in plug-in hybrid and performance (M) variants.
